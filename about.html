terrence o’connor

Originally from Massachusetts, Terrence (Terry) O’Connor began his career in New York City working at digital agency, Vayner Media, where he focused primarily on promotional strategy for brand campaigns. Simultaneously, Terry began helping his partner Benito Skinner (“Benny Drama”) build his career through his work on social media. With Terry behind the camera, Benny quickly became a viral sensation on Instagram and TikTok, and the couple moved to LA in March of 2020. A few months later, Terry went freelance and started his own business as a multi-faceted creative which has since evolved into promotional strategy, videography, photography, creative direction and producing social content. Utilizing the skills he had gained from his work with Benny, his innate knowledge of the internet, and love for all things pop culture, he began to work with artists and their teams across the entertainment industry where he has since garnered the attention of millions of viewers on TikTok for his clients, including; Charli XCX, A24, Caroline Polacheck, Noah Cyrus, HAIM, Orville Peck, Capitol Records, and more. Terry’s goals for the future are to continue focusing where he does his best work — having a unique ability to work directly with the artist, management, and label to marry the artist’s vision to their team’s marketing strategy with his deep knowledge of how to gather and retain the attention of audiences around the world.
